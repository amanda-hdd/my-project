OUR SERVICES

Classic: A meticulous technique that involves applying an extension to each natural lash, offering a subtle, delicate result, perfect for a natural, everyday look.

Hybrid set: This method combines individual extensions with lighter lash tufts, creating a denser, voluminous appearance while preserving the light, natural look of the lashes.

Russian Volume: An advanced technique that involves the application of several ultralight extensions to each natural lash, delivering intense volume, a dense effect and a deeply glamorous look.

Microblading: A technique used to structure eyebrows with a hair-by-hair effect. We use a stylus to imitate a fine, natural hair.

Shading or "powder brow": A technique that creates a powdery effect, similar to a pencil line, but prettier and more structured. It's a technique that allows you to achieve beautiful shading and gradation. Depending on your wishes, the scarred look can be very natural or more sophisticated, just like a make-up.

Microshading or "combo": A technique that combines microblading and shading. It enables the head of the eyebrow to be drawn hair by hair, and the tail to be drawn with a powdered effect.
